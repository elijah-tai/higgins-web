About

It definitely has its perks, but living with members can be tough sometimes - not everyone is super clean, and not everyone remembers to do shared chores that had been previously agreed upon.

Higgins is a dead-simple tool to keep members off of each others' nerves by automating the repetitive task of reminding your members to do their share of the work around the house.

We hope it's as useful to you as it is to us.
